Studying in Italy


I studied Master's degree in Italy. It was a great adventure for me. I always loved Italy. Only the culture itself is mesmerizing. I spent three years in Italy. It was a great experience for me. I had very colourful friendships. We used to cook for each other, and have cocktail parties and outdoor sports.

One of the main draws of studying in Italy is the opportunity to immerse yourself in a new culture. From learning the language to exploring the country's museums and landmarks, studying in Italy offers endless opportunities for personal growth and enrichment. Specifically in Rome, Everything is like an open museum. Most of the time, you discover something new. After a point, it draws you. Because you feel like you have to see everything and in a city like Rome, there is no end. You can never be satisfied with what you have seen. The city is huge and it’s impossible to see everything in one visit. Rome is an ancient city that has been around for centuries, so there are endless things to discover and learn about. It’s definitely worth your time if you want to study abroad in Italy.

Italy is also home to some of the world's oldest and most prestigious universities, including the University of Bologna and the University of Padua. These institutions offer a wide range of courses and degree programs, from art and design to business and economics. My university was Sapienza University. It is a good university with a good ranking. However, the English accent was a disadvantage for me. Because I had a hard time understanding the Italian professors. I can't say I didn't learn anything. I have really good lectures and lecturers.

Another perk of studying in Italy is the chance to travel throughout Europe. With its central location, Italy is a great starting point for exploring neighbouring countries and experiencing different cultures. Plus, with budget airlines and train systems, travel within Italy and Europe is often affordable and accessible. Of course, studying in Italy isn't without its challenges. One potential hurdle is the language barrier. While many Italians speak English, it's important to have at least a basic understanding of Italian to fully immerse yourself in the culture and make the most of your experience. I start Italian courses in CPIA. It is a government Italian course for immigrants. It is not the best one of course but it was free. So with all my friends, we start this course. I wasn't so focused on the Italian classes. The teacher was really nice but I had a Master's degree, a waiter job...

As soon as I moved there, I start working in an Irish Pub. Because I had to work. It was a job with late night shifts and very hard at the moment because my apartment was extremely far away. I managed to find only that job. Later on, I had a scholarship as well. In the beginning, I couldn't manage to apply for the scholarship before the deadline. Overall, It was a nice experience for me because I met many people from different countries. But this work is totally for another blog.

Another challenge can be adjusting to a new academic system. Italian universities may have different grading systems and teaching styles than what you're used to, so it's important to do your research and prepare accordingly. Most of the time, You don't understand If it is good great or bad. What is the average score in the class? Who is the best one? You will be lost among those questions. You only should focus on passing the exam.

Overall, studying in Italy is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity that offers both academic and personal growth. Especially for personal growth. You will learn and experience many different things. Overall, I was very stressed because I couldn't find my dream job. But when you look at the aspect of having fun and focusing on the beauty (things that I can't manage to do easily), It is definitely a must to do. If you're considering studying abroad, add Italy to the top of your list!

Finding a Job in Italy


I studied Master's degree in Italy. I lived there for almost 3 years. Despite the disadvantages, I loved living there. From day one, I start looking for jobs in my field. As a non-native Italian, finding a job in Italy can be a daunting task. I have faced numerous difficulties in securing employment. Employers often prioritize native speakers, even when the job does not necessarily require it.

First of all, I studied Communication and management. I know it is not the best practice to find a job in a foreign country. Because basically, you have to speak the language. Specifically, I tried to find a marketing position. I did my best to improve my skills and learn Italian. But in Italy, employers mostly look for a native speaker. In the different fields, the situation is different. I accept in my field finding a job is difficult in every country. For example, it is easier to find a position in computer science, engineering etc. Because basically, they speak job-related languages.

Additionally, navigating the Italian job market can be confusing and overwhelming. There are many bureaucratic hurdles to overcome, and the process can take much longer than expected. This is particularly challenging when you are living in a foreign country and trying to establish yourself.

Despite these challenges, I remain determined to find employment in Italy. I am committed to improving my language skills and networking with professionals in my field. I believe that my unique perspective as a non-native Italian can bring valuable diversity and creativity to any workplace. I applied for jobs as soon as I started my Master's degree. I tried to find simple jobs to earn some money. I looked for positions at the university. Because I heard there were some part-time positions. But then I found out all of them were only in Italian. Of course, then I start applying for waiter jobs in the tourist areas in Rome. I thought they must have been speaking in English. I was right about it. I started working in an Irish pub.

Of course, I can't say that it was what I have been dreaming of. However, It was a great experience. I am not just saying that but I learned about foreign consumers. It has improved me somehow. But I have to say that I wasn't expecting to work there for almost 2 years. It wasn't a solid 2 years. I got lucky sometimes and I found other positions in marketing and I worked remotely. But like I said I was lucky to find those. I can say that after that many years of job searching, I basically know who hires only with English in Rome. Yes, of course, they reject me.

All my Italian friends kept telling me "You should try Milan, Milano", and "They hire ex-pats". I applied for millions of positions in Milano as well. All I got was scammers and very very bad positions. I have to explain one of them. One of the employers called me directly and said " you applied for that position, we want to meet with you and discuss it". I said, "Great, let's schedule a meeting". They said let's meet this Saturday. It is odd but okay. Then we met remotely. There was a guy who is smoking, and he said "I don't speak English, my friend will connect in a second, he speaks". He connected and he was in the car and tried to speak with me. He basically said that "Normally, we were working with an American company but we don't at the moment. We will start working with another American company but I don't know anything at the moment so I can't explain the job.". I said, "Okay, I understand. But at least, money, contract, how I will work, what is the position, what I will do etc.". He said, "I can't tell you anything but there is no contract no salary only commission" and I said, "goodbye". After that day, I stop applying for jobs in Italy.

Needless to say, It is really hard to find a job in my field without Italian. I didn't even meet with the people in my situation who finds jobs. I am just an example. It might be my bad luck. In the end, I hope that Italy will recognize the talents and potential of non-native speakers like myself and provide more opportunities for us to contribute to the workforce.

AI for Marketing

I don’t think the world needs one more AI article or blog, but I have things to say as well. Everybody knows it and it is not that hard to learn because it learns for you. Artificial Intelligence, even Microsoft starts doing it. I guess they started some time ago, but it is never late. In this article, yes let’s call it an article, I will explain AI marketing techniques that may be useful for some of us.

First of all, I need to tell you that I wrote my thesis on “AI in Organizational Communication”. I mostly explained AI methods that have been used in Communication mediums. I mainly gather my argument along with the marketing techniques. When I was writing my thesis there wasn’t Chat GPT, so I proudly can say that I didn’t use it to write my thesis. But this is not the question anymore. The question is “Would it be even better even I would write it with Chat GPT?”.

Marketing is one of the most affected by AI tools. Using AI in Marketing is not such a new thing. AI has been tried many times in market research with real data to track consumer preferences and behaviours. There are many examples like “AI predicted that X consumer going to buy this product and that happened”. Machine Learning makes the magic. If you train the AI with as much data as you can, It will be stronger. With the recent updates in the AI field, people are using it every minute and AI keeps learning. As far as I remember, AI used to be good at numbers but not so efficient with text. Nowadays, with Chat GPT 4, It is even understanding memes and jokes.

I have to accept, before writing this article, I asked Chat GPT for some suggestions. Even with too many typos you can understand it is me because I only ask for some suggestions about the general structure. I ask it to analyse my website and my content and give me some examples according to them. It does a brilliant job. I used the Google sheet Chat GPT extension to do it. It is more convenient and It looks more professional.

For example, Chat GPT told me to start with “By 2025, the global market for AI in marketing is expected to reach $13.4 billion” to highlight the importance. There are many investments in this field because it is affecting everything. It is easy to use now, everybody can learn… Besides that, Open AI was an open source. They were a non-profit company. Their goal was to unleash the power of AI for all Humanity. AI is truly a power. Open AI tries to eliminate unfair competition. They were successful as well. However, in 2020, they became a for-profit. Microsoft bought some shares, and they are becoming bigger.

In marketing, AI found a huge usage area. You can do branding, market research, content marketing, online marketing, social media marketing etc. You can even find marketing strategies, create photos, and directly promote photos. In the early stage of this technology, like IBM Watson studio, you could use machine learning techniques to predict future consuming activities or classify text contents like consumer feedback. It was very limited and it wasn’t entirely successful in the text format. Of course, it could generate basic bar charts or suggestions according to the entered data. The data was mostly the one that you enter. However, AI can browse through the internet and find more personalized and accurate answers for you.

AI tools for Marketers

Nowadays, There is too many AI tool for Marketers to increase work quality and decrease the time spent. I will try to list some trending AI tools that Marketers can use in their work professionally.

  • Pebblely.com: It is a tool that can be used for Branding. It is specialized in brand photos.
  • Merlin: It is a google chrome extension that works with Chat GPT. It can help you write emails, social media posts etc. It works like a digital assistant
  • Validatorai: You have an idea about starting a business. This website can give you business ideas, give feedback about your business idea or it can explain to you the steps to be taken through your business idea.
  • Synthesia: it is a video generator platform. You can give the script and it can give you the video. It is simple but it is not free. It supports more than 120 languages.
  • Durable: It is a website-building AI. However, it helps you to build a website, track leads, organize invoices, get paid directly via durable (only in the USA) or analyse your online marketing performance. It is a perfect answer for online marketing needs.
  • Logo AI: This crazy AI website can give you many logo ideas for your business. You can personalize it. It is not free as well.
  • GPT for Sheets: It is a great feature to use google sheets with AI power. Basically, it is very useful for SEO and marketing Analyzing practices. You can generate tags according to your website. It has a huge impact on data cleansing. You don’t need to know codes to organize data and clean it.
  • Copy.ai: It is a website to help you write text, blogs, and articles. It is very user-friendly. You can save all your ideas. You can decide the tone that you want to use in your text. It might be friendly, professional, formal, etc. For starters, it is free up to some generated words.
  • Notion.ai: It is like the combination of Copy.io and merlin. It works like an assistant, on the other hand, it can generate your text. It makes a huge work on creating and organizing content.
  • Celtra.com: It is a great platform where you can generate many Advertising designs and they are all ready to share. Many international companies are already using it.
  • Syllaby.io: It is an AI-powered website that helps you with Social media content strategy. You can search through trending topics in your market and it gives you many options in those topics. It enables you to choose one and It generates you auto script about the topic. Also, you can keep track of your social media postings on this website. It is very useful but it has only a 7-day trial package for free.
  • Repurpose.io: It is definitely a great tool If you are practising more than one social media platform and nowadays, everybody does. This website links all your platforms and shares the content on all the different platforms. It is very helpful. Because when you share something on one platform, it might be even a podcast that you share on Spotify, it shares from other platforms spontaneously.
  • Adept: Their motto is “new way to use computer”. It works like a personal assistant, but You don’t need to click or login anything. The live example they said is great. For example, you said that “ can you add those leads to salesforce and tag them with a flag..” It logins to salesforce, creates new leads, and puts in all the information you provide. Basically, you don’t need to click anything.
  • Predis.ai: It is a very simple but effective tool for social media marketing. You should just enter some text about what you want and It generates directly shareable social media posts. Yet, you can schedule those posts.
  • Adcreative.ai: It creates you much content for your ads or social media. It is more personalized. You can upload your website, motto, colours and many other things. It is very user-friendly.
  • You.com: It is an AI-powered website that provides you with many different tools from coding to blogs. It is very useful if you want to generate some ideas. It works like Chat GPT. These are generated by you.com:

    “Chatbots: AI-powered chatbots can be used to interact with customers, answer their queries, and provide personalized recommendations. They can also help collect customer data and provide valuable insights to businesses.

    Content generators: AI-powered content generators can be used to create blog posts, social media content, and other marketing materials. These tools use natural language processing (NLP) algorithms to analyze data and generate high-quality content in a matter of minutes.

    Predictive analytics: AI-powered predictive analytics tools can help businesses identify trends, predict customer behaviour, and optimize marketing campaigns. These tools use machine learning algorithms to analyze large amounts of data and provide insights that can help businesses make informed decisions.

    Personalization engines: AI-powered personalization engines can be used to create personalized customer experiences. These tools use data such as browsing history, purchase behaviour, and social media activity to provide personalized product recommendations and content.

    Voice assistants: AI-powered voice assistants such as Alexa and Google Assistant can be used to engage with customers conversationally. These tools can help customers find products, make purchases, and get personalized recommendations.”

  • Adobe Firefly: It is a new tool Adobe created. It is a web-based program that everybody uses easily. There is only a beta version at the moment. But the features are very promising. It has features like midjourney but you can choose very different formats. The content it provides is more adjustable.
  • Adobe Podcast: It uses AI power to increase the quality of audio. It is very useful for podcasts. For this reason, might be useful for marketing needs.
  • Tinywow: It is a very useful webpage that combines very different tools that might be an answer to several needs.

Chat GPT is the most efficient and free tool that everyone can use. However, you need to learn how to write a prompt. Yes, it is easy. It will answer under any circumstances but if you learn you can get better and more realistic outcomes. Learnprompt.org is a great place to learn about prompts. You should check that out.

Most of those things are AI-powered tools some of them are very poor some of them extraordinarily useful. But most definitely, the way that we are doing business is changing maybe it is already changed. I gather websites that might be useful for marketers. But every other day there is more and more AI-powered tool. Additionally, Keep in your mind that, there are other analysing methods that they already use AI power for their dashboards.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the use of AI in marketing is becoming increasingly popular and essential for businesses of all sizes. With its ability to predict consumer behaviour, generate personalized content, and streamline marketing processes, AI is a powerful tool for marketers. Thanks to recent advancements, AI can now even understand jokes and memes, making it even more adaptable to the constantly evolving digital landscape. There are numerous AI tools available for marketers to use, such as Pebbly.com for branding, Validatorai for business ideas, and Durable for website building and online marketing. Additionally, Google Sheets Chat GPT extension, Copy.io, and Notion.ai are all great tools to help generate content and streamline the content creation process. As AI continues to evolve, it will be exciting to see how it will transform the marketing landscape even further.

In the end, AI is not a magical solution for everything. You should know what you are doing. At least you should know how to start. You should know how to use them quickly because it is an opportunity for every size of business. As a marketer, I am worried but on the other hand, those tools are for me and I can use it. It might take our business or it might make everything easier. There are many ethical dilemmas. I still think that even if you are using them, you have to have a strategy and idea about what you need and how you should do it. I know Chat GPT can give those answers but It is up to you to personalize those answers. I don’t know who but someone says that “ You can find the answers but this is the era of knowing how to ask better questions.”
